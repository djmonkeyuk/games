> **INFO**: This is an early prototype and is not a released game.

Neon Circuit is a tile-placement game about building cyberpunk style cities filled with vibrant neon lights and colours. Discover hidden combo scoring techniques and their effects on the city to reach a high score, then explore and admire your city using the many camera and filter options!

<!-- **[ [PLAY PROTOTYPE ON ITCH IO](https://djmonkeyuk.itch.io/neon-bloom) ]** -->

## Key Features

- A casual tile placement game with simple mechanics with an atmospheric futuristic style.
- Combo scoring techniques that are discovered through gameplay.
- Creative mode provides a relaxing and endless experience.
- Camera and filter options to tweak the aesthetic.
