Hello everyone!

Life has been turned upside down with the new baby that arrived a few weeks ago, but I'm happy to announce now that prior to her arrival I had been brewing up the Steam store pages for my completely new game I've been developing.

I'll be coming from a large-scale, life-sim, fantasy game like Tales from The Dancing Moon to a more focused experience of a sci-fi, city-building, card game that I'm calling... Neon Pulse 🌆

Thanks to the work I have done on TFTDM - a lot of foundational work is already in place, which means the development runtime will (hopefully) be a lot shorter. Localisation will also be a big focus of this game - where more people around the world can enjoy what it has to offer.

Things are early days still, and will be a relatively slow burn as I juggle priorities between life and game-dev. But I'm happy to already show a small glimpse of what's in store and start building interest and Wishlists!

Head over to the Steam page to learn more and give it a Wishlist - it would really mean a lot!
